Troubleshooting

  1. Restart the app: swipe up from the bottom, close the app, then reopen.
  2. Check permissions: ensure Health access and Files access are granted if needed.
  3. Free storage: large exports require free storage space. Remove unused files/apps.
  4. Try a smaller export: export fewer workouts to test.
  5. Contact support if the issue persists (include logs or screenshots if possible).

Tip: Reinstalling the app can fix corrupted installs.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

WorkoutExport does not collect personal data on its own servers. The app may request access to Apple Health to read workouts for export. Health data access happens locally on your device and is used only to perform the requested export.

Data Sharing

The app does not transmit your data to the developer or third parties unless you explicitly choose to export/share it using iOS share destinations (e.g., Files, iCloud Drive, Mail).
